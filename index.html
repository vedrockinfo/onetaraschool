The Vision of One Tara School

One Tara School is a uniquely conceived project. It aims to honor the past by nurturing the future. It is believed that ancestors bejewel the sky to shine upon us and light our path as stars or “tara.” It’s comforting to know that someone is looking over us.

Honoring a Legacy

One Tara School has been founded in memory of Dr. Jagdish Gandhi. He was the FounderManager of City Montessori School, Lucknow. He and his wife, Dr. Bharti Gandhi, started the school on borrowed capital of Rs 300. It has become the world's largest school. Dr. Jagdish Gandhi passed away on January 22, 2024. He left behind a legacy in education that is unsurpassed. It is in his memory that One Tara School has been started.

A Beacon of Hope

One Tara School is a free school for children and adolescent girls. It serves those who have been deprived of an education. Millions of children lack the comfort of knowing someone is looking over them. They fight their way on the streets all alone, with no hope. One Tara School gives this hope.

One Tara School

Richness of a culture is embedded in its heritage. From the Wag Festival in Africa to Dia de Muertos in North America and from Bon Festival in Japan to Pitri Paksha in India, we all honor and remember the departed in our own ways. It's a common belief that ancestors die to become a star or “tara” to bejewel the sky. They shine upon us and light our path even on the darkest of nights.

It's comforting to know that there is someone looking over us however this comfort is deprived from millions of children fighting their way on the street all alone with no hope. One Tara project under the flagship of DEVI Sansthan (Dignity Education Vision International) is an earnest effort to combat illiteracy and hunger.

A healthy mind resides within a healthy body. To move children a step towards learning foundational skills, we have collaborated with The Akshaya Patra Foundation to feed children nutritious meals that will motivate children to come to school every day. Also, through the incorporation of the groundbreaking new pedagogy, ALfA—Accelerating Learning for All, the dream to achieve Foundational Literacy and Numeracy among the underserved, will surely scale up learning among all children. The children will receive well-planned evening classes, to dress in crisp uniform and get essential stationery to plunge into their learning journey. Their dream to experience classroom, friendship and progress becomes a reality with your little help.

'What can I do?' the dilemma is over. Donate 999 per month in remembrance of your loved ones. Let your donation allow the stars above help shine the stars below.
